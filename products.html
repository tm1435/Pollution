How we use Excel and Access.

At our business, we take our organization very serioursly. By making sure all of our events are planned perfect and making sure our business runs smoohtly we use Microsoft Excel and Access in a variety of ways.

We use excel to make spreedsheet to make sure we know who is suppose to be cleaning and when and where. If we didn't use this incrediable useful source then we woudld be lost and wouldn't know who is suppose to be at the cleaning spots. Also, by keepung track of when and what professionals are coming to help clean inside the water to make sure we keep up on the dates.
