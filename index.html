This company is a non-profit orgainzation that helps reduce the amount of pollution in the lakes and bodies of water. Lots of bodies of water are known for having large amounts of pollution in them, we are helping reduce that number by spreeding the knowledge about water pollution. It was founded in 2010 by Tyler Merrill.

EcoGuard, founded in 2010, is dedicated to restoring and preserving the natural beauty of our lakes. Our mission is to implement sustainable and effective solutions to combat pollution and invasive species, ensuring healthier ecosystems for future generations. By combining innovation and community collaboration, we strive to make every body of water cleaner and more vibrant.

I truly enjoy collaborating with my team because their creativity and dedication inspire me every day. Working together allows us to share ideas, tackle challenges, and achieve our goals in ways that feel both meaningful and rewarding. The mutual respect and support we share create a positive and motivating environment, making every project a fulfilling and enjoyable experience.
